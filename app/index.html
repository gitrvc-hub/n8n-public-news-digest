Workflow structure

The implementation is intentionally simple enough to explain in under a minute.

1
Schedule the run

A morning trigger starts the workflow automatically on a daily cadence.

2
Fetch public feeds

RSS or public endpoints provide source material from selected topics.

3
Normalize and filter

The workflow parses items, removes empties, and scores likely importance.

4
Summarize with AI

Each chosen item is rewritten into a concise operator-friendly summary.

5
Deliver the digest

A single clean briefing is sent to Telegram, Slack, or email.
